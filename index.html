Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 26, 2026

Hot & Cold Word Guessing ("the App") is a free daily word puzzle game. This policy explains what data the App handles and how it is used.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device:

  • Your guesses and scores for each daily puzzle
  • Game statistics such as games played, win streaks, and average guesses

You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the App.

Data Sent to Third-Party Services

When you submit a guess, the guessed word is sent to an AI-powered service (provided by Rork) to compute a semantic similarity score. No personal information is included in these requests — only the guessed word and the target word.

Personal Information

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no user accounts, no login, and no registration.

Analytics and Advertising

The App does not include any analytics tracking or advertising.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
